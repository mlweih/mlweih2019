Isn't Social Media just a lot of yelling now?

Are you concerned about getting started with Instagram because you think it means you have to slog away at a constant stream of inane food pics? Or you think that the only way forward online is to become a wailing political Twitter banshee? Good news! I'll only make those recommendations if it fits your communication style. Take 5 minutes and fill out my free worksheet to find out if you're an Observer, a Broadcaster, or an Advocate; and discover the foundation of your communication style - the first step to having a personal digital strategy.

Still not sure if I can help you?

No worries, I understand. There's a lot going on with the Internet right now, particularly regarding privacy, ethics, community building, and safety. It's a pretty overwhelming time to get started on your own, and it can feel like the whole thing is a minefield.

Here are just a few of the core questions I'll address as we work together:

  • How do I know what's safe to share online, and what's not?
  • Which Social Media platforms work well together, and which ones don't?
  • How can anyone keep up when it seems like there's a new channel every day?
  • Is there a way to share my ideas in a style that's intelligent, but also sounds human?
  • What are Hashtags, and how do I leverage them?

I'm committed to helping you feel secure and empowered by having an online presence, by learning how to navigate the systems they're built upon. Think of social media as a knowledge-sharing tool, like the world's most advanced textbook. When you understand the "language" it's written in, you can not only use it any way that feels right for you, but also write your own chapters.
