Isn't Social Media just a lot of yelling now?

If you're concerned about getting started with Instagram because you think it means you have to slog away at a constant stream of inane food pics, or you think that the only way forward online is to become a wailing political Twitter banshee, no worries! I'll only make those recommendations if it fits your communication style. Take 5 minutes and fill out my free worksheet to find out if you're an Observer, a Broadcaster, or an Advocate; and discover the foundation of your communication style - the first step to having a personal digital strategy.

Still not sure if I can help you?

No worries, I understand. There's a lot going on with the Internet right now, particularly regarding privacy, ethics, community building, and safety. It's a pretty overwhelming time to get started on your own, and it can feel like the whole thing is a minefield.

Think of it this way: in all other aspects of your life, you probably have a plan to keep track of what's going on. Why not create one for getting yourself online? Just look at any news outlet to see people fooling around online who had no real understanding of what they were tackling. It's not that they're dumb, it's that they got caught up in a system they've never been taught.

Here are just a few of the core questions I'll address as we work together:

  • What are Hashtags, and how do I leverage them?
  • How do I know what's safe to share online, and what's not?
  • How can Social Media help me network in a way that feels like me?
  • How can anyone keep up when it seems like there's a new channel every day?

I'm committed to helping you feel secure and empowered by having an online presence, by learning how to navigate the systems they're built upon. To me social media is a knowledge-sharing tool; essentially the world's most advanced textbook. When you understand the "language" it's written in, you can not only use it any way that feels right for you, but also write your own chapters.
